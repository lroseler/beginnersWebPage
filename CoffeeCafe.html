Coffee Cafe

Come enjoy classic flavors and traditional techniques of making the perfect cup of caffine.

Perfectly roasted coffee beans that are only ground daily and just right for your specific taste.
We serve everything - from a regular cup of joe to fancy french press to delicious lattes.
Come visit us if you need a little perk in your day or just want a place to relax.

Location

Downtown Atlanta

Hours

Everyday (morning - evening)

Some tastey examples of what we have to offer!

  • Caramel flavor and espresso
  • Pumpkin Spice
  • Refreshing Peach Ice Tea
  • Iced Coffee
  • Special Brewed Dutch Coffee

Here are the steps to take to get the best coffee for you!

  1. Choose your drink type (plain coffee, latte, tea)
  2. Choose your size
  3. Pick your favorite milk (skim, whole, non-dairy)
  4. Let us know any other specifics (iced, hot, extra sweet)

Some Common Ingredients included in our popular drinks!

Caramel Latte
Shots of espresso, your choice of milk, and tons of caramel
Cafe Au Lait
Lots of Coffee, and equal amount of steamed milk
Jittery
Good coffee AND espresso (great for all-nighters)
Macchiato
Two shots of espresso, and only a "mark" of milk
